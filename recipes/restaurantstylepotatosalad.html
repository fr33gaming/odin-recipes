Restaurant Style Potato Salad

Description

This is not your ordinary potato salad. This salad steps up the game from ordinary salad. The flavors in this salad will blow your mind and ruin any other potato salad you come across in the future. The recipes blends looks and flavor for an impressive combination that appeals to the eyes and the mouth. Do not just take my word for it, make it for yourself and you can be the judge.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of Russet Potatoes
  • 1 Cup of Mayonnaise
  • 4 teaspoons of Sweet Pickle Relish
  • 4 teaspoons of White Sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of Chopped White Onion
  • 2 teaspoons of Prepared Mustard
  • 1 teaspoon of White Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of Minced Celery
  • 1 teaspoon of Minced Pimento
  • 1/2 teaspoon shredded Carrot
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Dried Parsely
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, and chop.
  2. In a large bowl, combine potatoes, mayonnaise, sweet pickle relish, sugar, onion, mustard, vinegar, celery, pimentos, carrot, parsley, pepper, and salt. Mix well, chill and serve.
